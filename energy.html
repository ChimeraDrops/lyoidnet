FW Consulting, Power Solutions

Co-founded an independent advisory firm to help Oklahoma policymakers understand natural gas-to-electricity conversion, microgrid technology, and decentralized power infrastructure. Operated entirely pro bono as a community service.

  • Served as technical co-architect of Oklahoma SB 93 — the state's Distributed Energy Resources & Behind-the-Meter Generation Act — authored by Senator Prieto and Representative Boles; legislation passed committee with a "Do Pass" recommendation in February 2025
  • Presented twice to the Oklahoma State Capitol on energy infrastructure strategy, wellhead gas conversion economics, and distributed power market development
  • Provided technical and commercial foundation for state-level energy policy decisions at the intersection of natural gas production and electricity markets
Oklahoma SB 93 (2025) Legislative Co-Development Senate Energy Committee — Do Pass, Feb 27, 2025

Establishes provisions for distributed energy resources (DERs) and microgrid generation, with a focus on behind-the-meter electric generation and Corporation Commission authorization for line construction in public rights-of-way. Authored by Sen. Dana Prieto and Rep. Brad Boles. FW Consulting provided the technical framework underpinning the bill's distributed generation definitions and behind-the-meter provisions.
