Parameter Value Notes
Unit footprint — Length × Width (bare unit)

Envelope dimensions — Total length × total width per unit
Min units (MW math) — Total MW ÷ MW per unit, rounded up (ceiling)

Total units — Min required + N+ spares
Total installed MW capacity — Total units × MW per unit
Total square footage — Total units × unit envelope sq ft
